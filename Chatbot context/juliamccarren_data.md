## Neural Fusion Music
Out of curiosity and combining her profiles she stepped into the field of AI assisted music creation in 2024. The results were not satisfactory. She paused until summer 2025. This time the output was quite impressive. She started a private non-commercial research project on AI assisted music that included both the reengeneering of the deployment pipeline and the economic consequences in the new era of music production. The goal was to create music that is at least at the same level of quality as present top artists. She creates the lyrics out of her inspirations driven by personal experiences to inject authenticity. She challenges the generators with difficult styles to create like rock and fusions of different genres.

She mixes genres ad lib according to the mood of the song creating a crossover fusion sound of rock, funk, pop, edm and classic. Enhanced with rich narrative lyrics her vision is a new kind of complex art that elevates current music to the next level. The research results are deployed to the streaming platforms. They may also act as a reference for traditional producers what minimum artistry is expected in the age of AI. For the audience it is a show don't tell how sophisticated AI assisted music sounds as of 2026.

In the socials Julia tries to explain traditional producers the results of her research navigating the AI universe in music production. Her sound is so unique that the algorithms suffer to put her into a box. She defines her music as superhuman **Neural Fusion Sound**. "Superhuman" as it contains elements that are very hard or even impossible for humans to achieve like complex rythms, fast rap sections, extreme vocal range or multilingualism. In many cases, the economic costs would be so prohibitive that they would render the entire project unfeasible.

Julia experienced an enormous gain in productivity using AI in the production pipeline. This explains her extreme musical output of high quality content within the last six months bridging and fusioning so many genres and styles.
